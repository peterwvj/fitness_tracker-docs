COLLECTION OF YOUR INFORMATION

The Application needs camera access to:

1. Scan barcodes, which are used to retrieve information about food
   products from the [[https://world.openfoodfacts.org/][Open Food Facts]] (OFF) third-party database and
   assisting users with entering barcodes for newly created OFF food
   products.

2. Take food product photos, which may optionally be attached to food
   products submitted to OFF.

The Application requires Internet access to:

1. Retrieve information about OFF food products, creating new OFF food
   products, and submitting changes to existing OFF food products.

2. Log in. The Application uses [[http://firebase.google.com/][Firebase]]/Social login to authenticate
   users and manage them. The Application will use information
   available via the social network accounts (name, profile image
   etc.) to show the user who is currently logged in to the
   Application.

The Application stores the following information on the device that it
runs on:

1. Meal plan related information provided by the user.

2. User settings/preferences.

3. OFF credentials (if provided by the user), which are used to
   authenticate against OFF when creating/editing OFF food
   products. OFF passwords are stored/protected with [[https://pub.dev/packages/flutter_secure_storage][Flutter Secure
   Storage]].
